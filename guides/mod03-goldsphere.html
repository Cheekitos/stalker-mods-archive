Goldsphere [OGSR]

Installation Guide

  • Goldsphere OGSR 1.3 + All fixes by Toastman221 & Seushimare + Patch 1.4.3.5.
  • Requires Microsoft Visual C++ 2017, DirectX and OpenAL like many old-school mods.
  • Extract Archive, move game folder to the root of your drive.
  • Launch "Start game.bat" or "xrEngine.exe" in the bin_x64 folder.
