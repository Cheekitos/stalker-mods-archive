Dreamcatcher

Installation Guide

  • This is the older 22-11 version of the mod with full English translation.
  • Requires Dependencies found in "Essentials" at the top of the page.
  • Extract Archive, move game folder to the root of your drive.
  • Launch DC_Mod.exe, click on the launcher top button "ЗАПУСТИТЬ ИГРУ" (START GAME)
  • (Optional) No Tonemap & mapping effects - copy in main game folder
  • (Optional) Extra Ambiant Sounds - copy in main game folder
  • (Optional) Patch 07-12 - adds a lot of fixes and new cinematics, but all the English translations for this new content will be missing
